Mental Health Statistics (2021)

According to WHO, mental health disorders are prevalent globally. Below are some key insights:

  • Depression: Affects 28% of the world population.
  • Anxiety Disorder: 26.9% of people suffer from this condition.
  • Sleep Problems: 27.6% of people experience sleep issues.
  • Stress: 36.5% of people suffer from stress.
  • Post-Traumatic Disorder: Affects 24.1% of individuals.
  • Psychological Distress: Impacts 50% of people globally.
  • Depression in Adults: 5% of the adult population suffers from depression.
