How to Support Someone with

Offering support to individuals facing mental health difficulties can be incredibly impactful. Below are some effective strategies to help those in need:

  • Listen without Judgment: Offer a non-judgmental space for them to express their emotions. Avoid offering immediate solutions or dismissing their feelings. Simple statements like "I’m here for you" or "Tell me more about what you’re going through" can create a supportive environment.
  • Encourage Professional Help: Suggest seeking guidance from a qualified mental health professional, such as a therapist or counselor. In urgent cases, provide hotline information, such as:
    • Nepal National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1166
  • Offer Practical Assistance: Help with daily tasks such as grocery shopping, running errands, or arranging transportation to therapy sessions. These small acts of kindness can significantly reduce their stress.
  • Check-In Regularly: Consistent communication, such as sending a text or making a brief phone call, can help them feel supported and less isolated. Even brief check-ins show you care.
